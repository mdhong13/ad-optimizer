If something unusual happens to you, a message will be sent.

An app running in the background detects smartphone usage.
If smartphone usage cannot be detected in power-saving mode, you will receive a notification. Please tap the notification to open the app.
If your smartphone is not used for 12 hours, an SMS will be sent to the designated number.


"On January 19, John did not use his smartphone for 12 hours. Please call to check on him."


One free message is provided each week. After the free message is used, additional messages can be purchased.
To send SMS messages to multiple numbers, additional messages are required. One additional message is used for each SMS sent.
Safety Message sends a daily reassurance message at 6 PM using one additional message.


"Today, January 19, John is doing well."
